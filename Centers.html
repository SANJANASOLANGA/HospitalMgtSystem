Diabetes Center


The following are the high risk factors
  • Family history of Diabetes.
  • Age over 45 years.
  • Diabetes during pregnancy or delivering baby > 9 pounds.
  • Prolonged use of certain medication
Common Causes
  • Obesity.
  • Unhealthy eating habits.
  • High stress.
  • Frequent passing of urine.
  • Increased thirst.
  • Lethargy.
  • Numbness of toes and feet.
If you have any of the above symptoms, have your diabetes screening done at Aragi Diabetic Center today, as tomorrow may be too late.

Prevention of Diabetes.
  • Healthy Diet.
  • Stress Management.
  • Regular Exercise.
  • Regular checkups - will help you detect the pre-diabetes state, so you can take action to prevent yourself from going in to a diabetes state.

Kidney Care Center


Why are Kidneys so important to us?

The Kidneys despite being relatively small organs (about the size of an adult fist), do tremendously important functions that help humans function.

The main functions of the kidneys;
  1. Excretion of waste such as toxins, excess salts, urea and Creatinine.
  2. Filtration, Re-absorption, and Secretion of blood cells, large molecules, toxins, fluid and minerals.
  3. Water Homeostasis
  4. Acid/Base Homeostasis
  5. Electrolyte Homeostasis - Sodium, Potassium, Chloride, Calcium, Magnesium
  6. Blood Pressure Homeostasis
  7. Secretion of Hormones such as calcitrol and erythropoietin

Liver Center


What is the liver?
The liver is the largest glandular organ of the body. It weighs about 3 lb (1.36 kg).

The liver lies on the right side of the abdominal cavity beneath the diaphragm.A healthy liver is the key to achieving a healthy life. So it is important that you maintain the good health of your liver at all times.

It serves as
  1. Body's engine - feeds the body with energy.
  2. Pantry - storing iron, copper, vit. A & D and several B vitamins.
  3. Refinery - filtering blood of toxins, breaking down & recycle red blood cells.
  4. Food processor - producing bile, urea, cholesterol.
  5. Garbage disposal - disposing of toxic materials.
  6. Trouble shooter - regulates sex hormones, cholesterol levels , mineral supplies and wardoff viruses and infections.
  7. Guardian angel - protects your body every day with clock work precision.
Causes of liver disease
  • Congenital birth defects or abnormalities of the liver present at birth.
  • Metabolic disorders, like diabetes & obesity
  • Viral infections
  • Alcohol
  • Certain medications that are toxic to the Liver
  • Trauma or injury
