Dementia Clinic

Dementia is an irreversible organic brain disease causing memory /personality disorders, deterioration of personal care, impaired cognitive ability and disorientation. Patient assessments and management is done at the Dementia Clinic.

Exercise ECG

He patient undergoes a standardized (Bruce) protocol on increasing exercise with continuous 12 lead ECG and blood preasure monitoring and watches for depressions. This is used to diagnose IHD (Ischemic Heart Disease), assessment of exercise tolerance etc.
